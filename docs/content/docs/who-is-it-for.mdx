**Founders & small teams** (top row) — you're a founder or have a small team of ~1–25. You need answers from your data without a large data team.

**Data teams** (bottom row) — you're a data team supporting various teams' data needs. You need self-service, governance, and trust.

---

## Founder + Simple data

> Just help me build a dashboard

**Who you are**: A founder, solo operator, or small team (~1–25 people) with a database, a CSV, or a Google Sheet. You don't have a data team and don't want one.

**What matters most**: Speed. Go from question to answer without ceremony.

**Key features**:
- [Explore](/docs/bi/explore) — ask questions in natural language, get instant results
- [Questions](/docs/bi/questions) — save and revisit your analyses
- [CSV / Google Sheets connections](/docs/data/connections) — upload a file and start querying immediately
- [Visualization](/docs/bi/visualization) — charts with zero configuration
- [Dashboards](/docs/bi/dashboards) — assemble a few charts into a shareable view

**Start here**: [Getting Started guide →](/guides/getting-started)

## Founder + Complex data

> I need real answers from our warehouse without a large data team

**Who you are**: A founder or small team with a real data warehouse (Postgres, BigQuery, etc.), multiple data sources, and SQL that's getting serious — but no dedicated data team to manage a traditional BI tool.

**What matters most**: Depth without overhead. The tool should handle complexity without requiring a BI admin.

**Key features**:
- [DuckDB / BigQuery / PostgreSQL connections](/docs/data/connections) — connect to your actual warehouse
- [Data Modeling](/docs/data/data-modeling) — create trusted, materialized datasets
- [Pivot tables](/docs/bi/visualization#pivot-tables) — cross-tabulation with formulas and heatmaps
- [Advanced viz settings](/docs/bi/visualization#general-viz-settings) — dual-axis, annotations, column formatting
- [Filters & Parameters](/docs/bi/filters) — dynamic, parameterized queries
- [MCP integration](/docs/integrations#mcp-model-context-protocol) — query your data from Claude Desktop or Cursor

**Start here**: [Your First Question →](/guides/first-question)

## Data team + Simple data

> I want ops and marketing to answer their own questions — and trust the answer

**Who you are**: A data team (or the one data person) at a growing company. The data isn't that complex, but you're drowning in ad-hoc requests. You need business teams to self-serve.

**What matters most**: Self-service that actually works. Non-technical users need to get answers without pinging you every time — and the answers need to be correct.

**Key features**:
- [Knowledge Base](/docs/knowledge-base/context) — teach the agent your team's metrics, conventions, and gotchas
- [Dashboards](/docs/bi/dashboards) — shared views with interactive filters
- [Text blocks](/docs/bi/dashboards#text-blocks) — add context and commentary alongside charts
- [Dashboard parameters](/docs/bi/dashboards#dashboard-parameters) — one filter controls all questions
- [Slack integration](/docs/integrations#slack) — let teams ask data questions right in Slack
- [Evals](/docs/knowledge-base/evals) — verify the AI gives correct answers before handing it to the team

**Start here**: [Creating a Dashboard →](/guides/creating-dashboard)

---

## Data team + Complex data

> Every number needs to trace back to a governed, trusted dataset

**Who you are**: A data team serving dozens or hundreds of stakeholders across the org, with governance requirements, multiple data sources, and a need for reliability. You've been burned by "vibe SQL" before.

**What matters most**: Trust, governance, and scalability. Every metric needs to be defined once, governed centrally, and used consistently.

**Key features**:
- [Data Modeling](/docs/data/data-modeling) — single source of truth with clear grain and business logic
- [Context hierarchy](/docs/knowledge-base/context#tree-based-whitelisting) — org-wide governance with team-level access control
- [Evals](/docs/knowledge-base/evals) — measure and track AI accuracy systematically
- [Proactive Analytics](/docs/proactive) — scheduled reports and threshold-based alerts
- [Permissions & modes](/docs/installation) — user management and environment isolation
- [MCP + Slack](/docs/integrations) — meet stakeholders where they work

**Start here**: [Knowledge Base Setup →](/guides/knowledge-base-setup)
