## Cloud vs Self-Hosted

| | Cloud / Managed | Self-Hosted |
|---|---|---|
| **Setup time** | ~2 minutes | ~5 minutes |
| **Hosting** | Managed by MinusX team | Your infrastructure |
| **Upgrades** | Automatic | Manual (pull + redeploy) |
| **Data residency** | Data stays in your DB/warehouse. BI layer on our servers. | Everything on your servers — data never leaves |
| **AI** | Hyper-tuned, customized models with best-in-class performance | Vanilla setup — bring your own API key, tune it yourself |
| **Scaling & Security** | Handled for you | You manage capacity and security |
| **Cost** | Subscription pricing | Free (open source) + your infra costs (**~$300-500/month**) + AI usage costs |
| **Best for** | Teams that want to get started fast without ops overhead | Trying out MinusX, or teams that need full control over their stack |
