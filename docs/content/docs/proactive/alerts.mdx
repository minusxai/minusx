Alerts monitor your questions and notify you when specific conditions are met.

## How alerts work

An alert watches a question's results on a schedule. When the results meet your defined condition, MinusX sends a notification.

## Example alerts

- "Notify me when daily revenue drops below $10,000"
- "Alert if error rate exceeds 5%"
- "Send a message when new sign-ups exceed 100 in a day"
