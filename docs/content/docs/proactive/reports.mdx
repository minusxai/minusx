Reports let you schedule dashboards or questions to be delivered automatically on a recurring basis.

## How reports work

A report takes a snapshot of a dashboard or question's results and delivers it — typically via email — on a schedule you define.

## Use cases

- **Daily standups** — send yesterday's key metrics every morning
- **Weekly summaries** — deliver a revenue dashboard to leadership each Monday
- **Monthly reviews** — generate end-of-month performance reports automatically
