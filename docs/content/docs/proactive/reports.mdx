title: Reports
description: Scheduled reports delivered to your team
---

Reports let you schedule dashboards or questions to be delivered automatically on a recurring basis. Instead of checking MinusX manually, the data comes to you.

---

## How reports work

A report takes a snapshot of a dashboard or question's results and delivers it — typically via email — on a schedule you define.

---

## Setting up a report

1. Open a dashboard or question
2. Select **Schedule Report** from the actions menu
3. Configure the schedule (daily, weekly, monthly)
4. Add recipient email addresses
5. Save

---

## Report contents

Reports include:

- The dashboard or question title
- A rendered snapshot of the visualization
- A link back to the live version in MinusX

---

## Use cases

- **Daily standups** — send yesterday's key metrics every morning
- **Weekly summaries** — deliver a revenue dashboard to leadership each Monday
- **Monthly reviews** — generate end-of-month performance reports automatically
