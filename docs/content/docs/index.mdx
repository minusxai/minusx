MinusX is an agentic, AI-powered business intelligence tool that helps teams explore data, build dashboards, and get answers from their databases — all through natural conversation.

---

## Why MinusX?

Traditional BI tools force you to choose between power and ease of use. SQL-based tools are flexible but require expertise. Drag-and-drop builders are accessible but limiting. MinusX bridges this gap with an AI assistant that understands your data, writes SQL, and builds visualizations — while keeping you in full control.

## How it works

MinusX uses a file-system based architecture. Every question, dashboard, and piece of context is a document stored in a structured file tree. This means:

- **Everything is versioned** — documents have clear save/edit states
- **Everything is organized** — folders and paths keep your workspace tidy
- **Everything is shareable** — share by ID, organize by team

<Callout type="info">
MinusX's AI reads your Knowledge Base before every query — the more context you provide, the better the results.
</Callout>
